Research

My research interest lies on learning/developing a robot that can accomplish complex, long-horizon manipulation tasks. Ultimately, I aim to develop a general-purpose robot system. To achieve my ultimate goal, specific topics I am interested in : 1. A model with a human-level physical understanding 2. Efficient rl algorithms 3. Contact-rich dexterous manipulation.
